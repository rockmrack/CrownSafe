Delete your BabyShield account

You can delete your account directly in the BabyShield app at any time.

In-app path

Open the app → Settings → Account → Delete my account.
You'll be asked to confirm. This action is permanent.

What happens when you delete

  • Your account and personal data are deleted from our systems.
  • Push notification tokens and device links are invalidated.
  • Backups and legally required records (e.g., payments/fraud prevention) may be retained as permitted by law and then purged on schedule.

Can't access the app?

If you can't sign in, contact support@babyshield.cureviax.com from your registered email and request deletion. We may ask for verification.

Data export

You can export your data in the app before deletion: Settings → Account → Export my data.

If you deleted by mistake, we may be unable to restore your data after processing completes.
