1. Introduction

Welcome to BabyShield ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle data. BabyShield is a consumer safety information service that provides access to product recall and safety data from government agencies worldwide.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

2. Service Purpose

BabyShield provides:

  • Access to product recall information from government safety agencies
  • Safety alerts and notifications about consumer products
  • Search functionality for recall and safety data
  • Visual product identification features

All information is sourced from official government databases and is provided for informational purposes only.

4. How We Use Information

We use collected information solely for:

  • Service Provision: Delivering recall and safety information
  • Support: Responding to your inquiries
  • Legal Compliance: Processing DSAR requests (export/deletion)
  • Service Improvement: Anonymous crash reporting and performance monitoring
  • Security: Protecting against abuse and unauthorized access

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We DO NOT:
  • Sell your personal data
  • Share data with advertisers
  • Use third-party analytics that track users
  • Engage in behavioral advertising

We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety: To protect the safety of users or the public
  • Service Providers: With trusted providers who help operate our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Data Storage & Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Storage: Secure cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest
  • Access Control: Limited to authorized personnel only
  • Retention:
    • DSAR records: 3 years (for legal compliance)
    • Support emails: 1 year
    • Crash logs: 90 days

9. Children's Privacy

BabyShield is a general audience service. While our content relates to child safety products, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or applicable age in your jurisdiction). If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete the information.
